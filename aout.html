Trimester 1: Weeks 1-12

  • Weeks 1-2: Preconception - Prepare with folic acid, check-up
  • Weeks 3-4: Confirmation - Pregnancy test, first appointment
  • Weeks 5-6: Symptoms Begin - Track morning sickness, fatigue
  • Weeks 7-8: First Scan - Early ultrasound, heartbeat
  • Weeks 9-10: Nutrition Focus - Iron, Vitamin D, light exercise
  • Weeks 11-12: Risk Screening - Nuchal scan, genetic tests

App Features: Symptom tracker, reminders, safe food guide, health tips
